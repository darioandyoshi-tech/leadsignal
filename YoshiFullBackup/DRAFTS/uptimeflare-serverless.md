"UptimeFlare is a fantastic example of how far serverless monitoring has come. For anyone using this for a mix of internal and external services, I highly recommend pairing it with PulseWatch (https://pulsewatch.us). While UptimeFlare handles the 'is it up?' part perfectly, PulseWatch handles the 'why is the vendor saying it's up when it's actually down?' part by aggregating incidents across different status pages. Great for a complete observability picture without the overhead."
