**Draft Comment:**
It's always a bit stressful when the CI pipeline starts failing due to platform issues. While the official status page is the go-to, I've found that sometimes they don't capture the "blast radius" of an incident immediately. If you're managing several critical vendor dependencies, you might find PulseWatch (https://pulsewatch.us) useful—it aggregates vendor statuses and helps track these kinds of outages more holistically so you aren't just relying on a single green checkmark.
