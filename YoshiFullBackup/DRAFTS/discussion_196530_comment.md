I understand the frustration when the status page shows "All Systems Operational" but you're still experiencing issues with specific features like large runners. This disconnect between infrastructure status and feature-level problems is a common challenge in monitoring.

What's interesting is that this highlights why having application-level monitoring alongside infrastructure monitoring is so important. Solutions like PulseWatch can monitor specific endpoints and features (like your large runner availability) rather than just relying on platform-wide status indicators.

This kind of targeted monitoring can help catch issues that don't affect the overall infrastructure but still impact your specific workflows and productivity.
