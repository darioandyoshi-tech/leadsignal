Hey! Fellow monitoring tool builder here.

Your content-first approach is smart, especially for a dev tool. We built PulseWatch (vendor outage monitoring, 1500+ services) and found that technical content + SEO was way more effective than cold outreach.

One thing that helped us: **positioning around the pain, not the product**. Instead of "cron monitoring tool," try "how to catch silent cron failures before they break production." Your articles are probably already doing this, but doubling down on the problem > solution framing worked well for us.

Also, r/sre and r/devops on Reddit are surprisingly receptive to monitoring tools if you lead with "here's what we learned" rather than "use our thing." We got our first 50 users from a single post about status page delays.

Zero users is normal at this stage. Keep shipping. The content compounds.
