Developers, QA engineers, support teams, and documentation authors use this skill to choose reliable screenshot capture methods for desktops, browsers, simulators, CI runs, bug reports, visual review, and before/after comparisons. <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Screenshots can expose secrets, personal data, internal URLs, notifications, account details, or other sensitive visible content. <br>
Mitigation: Capture only the needed area and review or mask sensitive content before sharing screenshots. <br>
Risk: Unstable page state, viewport changes, animations, cursors, timestamps, or randomized content can make screenshots misleading or unsuitable for comparison. <br>
Mitigation: Wait for the target state, fix viewport and theme settings, remove visual noise, and compare before/after screenshots under the same conditions. <br>
