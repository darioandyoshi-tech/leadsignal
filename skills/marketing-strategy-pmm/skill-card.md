### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated marketing plans and claims may contain incorrect market, competitor, customer, or performance assumptions. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as drafts and verify claims, market data, customer evidence, and attribution before publishing or using them in sales materials. <br>
Risk: Outreach, publishing, advertising, CRM, or budget changes could affect customers or spend if acted on through separate tools. <br>
Mitigation: Require explicit human approval before sending messages, publishing posts, changing ad budgets, updating CRM records, or contacting customers. <br>
Risk: International GTM and localization guidance may omit jurisdiction-specific privacy, legal, or compliance requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Route market-entry, tracking, outreach, and localization plans through regional privacy and legal review before execution. <br>
