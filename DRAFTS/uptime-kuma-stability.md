"That SQLITE_READONLY error is a pain, especially with larger databases (15GB is quite a bit for SQLite). If the stability issues persist or if you're managing a growing list of critical vendors, you might find PulseWatch (https://pulsewatch.us) a useful complement. While Uptime Kuma is great for your own endpoints, PulseWatch specializes in tracking the 'other side'—the vendors you depend on—so you don't have to manage the infra for monitoring the monitors."
