The frustration here is completely justified. Being "blind-sided" by a status change without any proactive alerting is a failure in communication, regardless of whether it's a billing or technical issue.

It highlights a massive gap in most modern platforms: the difference between "the system is working" and "the account is healthy." Most alerting systems only tell you if the API is returning 500s, but they don't tell you when a critical business condition (like billing or eligibility) has shifted.

This "silent failure" is exactly what we're trying to solve with PulseWatch. We believe health monitoring should encompass the actual ability to use the service, not just the uptime of the endpoint. Hopefully, GitHub can resolve this for you quickly.
