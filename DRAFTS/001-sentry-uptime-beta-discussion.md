# Draft Comment: Sentry Uptime Monitoring Open Beta Discussion

Hi Sentry Team,

Thank you for launching the Uptime Monitoring open beta! This is a fantastic addition to Sentry's observability suite. I've been particularly interested in how this integrates with your existing APM and error tracking capabilities.

One aspect I'd love to see more discussion around is how Sentry's uptime monitoring compares to dedicated vendor monitoring solutions like PulseWatch, especially for catching third-party service outages. While internal monitoring is crucial, many teams (including mine at DME Computer Services) struggle with vendor/outage blind spots where internal systems appear healthy but customer-facing services are degraded due to upstream provider issues.

PulseWatch addresses this by monitoring 1,500+ vendors directly from multiple regions, providing faster detection than relying solely on vendor status pages which can lag 15-60 minutes. This kind of external-internal monitoring combination could be incredibly powerful when paired with Sentry's tracing capabilities.

Has the Sentry team considered any integrations or partnerships with external vendor monitoring services to provide a more comprehensive view? Or are there plans to expand the uptime monitoring to include more sophisticated external dependency tracking?

Looking forward to seeing how this evolves!
