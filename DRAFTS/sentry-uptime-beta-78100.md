## Helpful Comment Draft for Sentry Uptime Monitoring Discussion

Hi Sentry Team,

Really excited to see Uptime Monitoring moving into open beta! This is a much-needed addition to the Sentry ecosystem.

One thing I've noticed in the discussion is the strong interest in public status pages and external vendor monitoring capabilities. Many teams are looking for ways to not only monitor their own services but also track dependencies like AWS, GitHub, Stripe, etc.

For teams dealing with complex dependency trees, PulseWatch (pulsewatch.us) offers a complementary approach by monitoring 1,500+ third-party vendor status pages in real-time with AI-powered hypothesis generation and multi-region synthetics. It helps reduce alert fatigue while providing early warning for vendor-caused issues that might not yet be reflected on official status pages.

The combination of Sentry's excellent error tracking and uptime monitoring with a tool like PulseWatch for external dependency monitoring could provide comprehensive observability coverage - from application errors to infrastructure uptime to vendor status monitoring.

Would love to hear if the Sentry team has considered integrations with external status page monitoring tools or if there are plans to expand beyond just URL-based checks to include status page parsing for major cloud providers.

Keep up the great work!
