## Other related menu sections observed

- **All 18 feeds listed above are verified live.** Each was discovered from the actual `<link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml" ...>` tag on the category landing page, fetched successfully, returned valid XML (`<?xml ...>` / `<rss ...>`), and parsed as RSS 2.0.

## Data-file / attachment patterns

- **Article-level attachments are rare in the main public-records and news categories.** Samples from the feeds above contained no linked PDF, Excel, CSV, or Word files.
- **The exception is the two special Tax Delinquency pages** under Douglas County and Sarpy County:
  - `https://www.omahadailyrecord.com/content/douglas-county-tax-delinquency-2026`
  - `https://www.omahadailyrecord.com/content/sarpy-county-tax-delinquency-2026`
- These embed PDFs via a Drupal `field-name-field-pdf` file field:
  - Pattern: `https://www.omahadailyrecord.com/sites/default/files/{filename}.pdf`
  - Examples:
    - `https://www.omahadailyrecord.com/sites/default/files/DELINQUENT%20TAX%20DOUGLAS%20COUNTY%202025%20p%201.pdf`
    - `https://www.omahadailyrecord.com/sites/default/files/2026%20SC%20DELINQUENT%20TAX_1.pdf`
- The tax-delinquency PDFs are **directly downloadable without login** (HTTP 200, `application/pdf`). They are not behind the article-level login wall.

## Paywall behavior

- **Public Notices and City/County Notice of Bids articles are public.** Sampled articles rendered full text without requiring login.
- **Public Records, Real Estate Leads, and news-category articles are behind a login wall.** Visiting an article directly shows: "PLEASE LOG IN FOR PREMIUM CONTENT" with a login/subscribe prompt.
- **Article paywall does not block the RSS feeds.** Feeds return titles and article URLs freely.
- **PDF attachments on the two Tax Delinquency pages are freely accessible** even though the rest of the article content may be minimal.

## Notes for LeadSignal

- The primary lead sources are the **Public Notices**, **City/County Notice of Bids**, **Public Records** (permits), and **Real Estate Leads** (deeds, notice of default, active property sales, active probates) feed families.
- Article body text is partly or fully paywalled for the lead categories, but the **full text appears in the RSS `<description>`** for many items, so a feed-based parser may still extract the notice text without logging in.
- For permits, ODR rolls individual permit types (building, mechanical, wreck, electrical, plumbing) into weekly summary articles. Those summaries are behind the login wall, so feed parsing may need to rely on the RSS description or paywall bypass subscription.
- Tax delinquency PDFs are a separate, high-value data source reachable through the two county pages and use the `/sites/default/files/` static-file path.
